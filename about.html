ABOUT AMBIKA ENTERPRISES



Ambika Enterprises is one of the leading Manufacturers and Exporter of Industrial Minerals i.e. Garnet Sand, Quartz Lumps, Grains & Powder, China Clay Lumps & Powder, Quick Lime, Hydrated Lime Powder, Potash Feldspar etc…. . Ambika Enterprises is an ISO 9001 certified company. We are able to cater to diverse requirements of our customers on timely basis. We follow stringent quality control measure at all levels of production i.e. from procurement of raw material to final the delivery. We strive to supply the best quality material, as per the needs of the customer for the specified grade.

All products are sorted, cleaned and processed with the aid of latest machinery as per international norms. Our exclusive range of minerals have always been appreciated for their accurate composition/purity and enjoys widespread application in areas such as Cosmetics, Pharmaceuticals, Paint industries, Pesticides, Rubber industries, and even Paper industries across India as well as many other countries in the Middle East, South East Asia and Europe.

Ambika Enterprises believes to achieve excellence with quality, where technology stretches to achieve timelines and where processes drive perfection.
